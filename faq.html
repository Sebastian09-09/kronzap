Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our automation services, process, and pricing.
What is the process for starting a new automation project?

Our process is simple and transparent. It starts with a free consultation to understand your needs. Next, we provide a detailed proposal with a project scope, timeline, and quote. Once approved, we move to development, with regular check-ins to show progress. Finally, we deliver the solution, provide training, and offer ongoing support.

How much do your custom RPA and bot development services cost?

The cost varies widely depending on the complexity of the project. For any custom bot, RPA workflow, or browser extension, we provide a detailed, fixed-price quote after our initial discovery call so you know the full cost upfront. We focus on delivering a high ROI for your investment.

Who owns the intellectual property of the final software?

Upon full and final payment, you, the client, own the full intellectual property rights to the custom software we develop for you. We only retain the rights to our pre-existing, reusable code libraries and frameworks to help us build efficiently.

Can you integrate with my existing CRM or other software?

Absolutely. Custom integration is one of our core services. We can build automation solutions that seamlessly connect with your existing software and platforms, whether they have a modern API or require more complex integration methods.

What kind of support do you offer after the project is completed?

We offer a standard post-launch support period to fix any bugs or issues. Beyond that, we provide flexible ongoing maintenance and support packages to ensure your automation continues to run smoothly, handle updates, and adapt to any changes in your business processes.

How long does it take to develop a custom bot or RPA solution?

Timelines depend on the project's scope. A simple data scraper or browser extension might take 1-2 weeks. A more complex RPA workflow or an integrated social media bot could take 4-8 weeks or more. We will provide a clear timeline in our project proposal.
